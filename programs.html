    Introductory courses

    DURATION: 1/2 and 1 day

    Our introductory training sessions are designed for people with limited knowledge or experience in behaviour change theory and practice. Ideal for people working in non-profits, government, social enterprises and community organisations.

    Advanced courses (Masterclasses)

    DURATION: 1/2 and 1 day

    Our advanced training courses build on the behaviour change fundamentals covered in our introductory sessions. Masterclasses have a strong practical focus and allow participants to apply what they’ve learned to real-world problems.

    Organisational Capacity Building

    DURATION: N/A

    We work with organisations in Australia and overseas to build their skills, knowledge and capacity to drive change. We can tailor our off-the-shelf training to meet your specific needs, or work with you to develop and run workshops where we delve into the specific issue you are trying to address, or future you’re trying to create.

    What sets our training apart?

    Practical expertise

    Our trainers have a minimum 20 years’ experience ‘practicing what they teach’. They have applied their knowledge to deliver successful behaviour change interventions in Australia and overseas.

    Multi-disciplinary approach

    We draw on a range of disciplines to achieve behavioural change and understand what approach (or combination of approaches) will give your behaviour change program the best chance to succeed.

    Breadth of application

    The knowledge and skills gained by participants can be used to address behavioural challenges in health, social and environmental contexts. We know because we’ve used them ourselves.

    We're pracademics

    We work closely with academic institutions in Australia and overseas. Our training content draws on the evidence base, and our own experience translating academic research across numerous disciplines into meaningful action.
